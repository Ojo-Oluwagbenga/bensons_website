Our Story

Bensons – A Non-Profit Organization

Welcome to Bensons — a collective of committed individuals building open research facilities for global transformation in healthcare, education, and food systems. More than an initiative, Bensons is a mission-driven movement inspired by both science and faith.

Our Mission

To build accessible, open facilities that support holistic growth for individuals and communities.

  • Nurturing both physical and spiritual well-being
  • Driving collaborative and community-led development

Labs Projects

OpenBox

OpenBox is a public storage and delivery system that functions like a digital-age postal box. It features smart lockers for secure package deposits, pickups, and even document printing. Designed to serve communities, especially those without traditional access to logistics or printing services.

Status: In Dev

View Project
Flip Art

Flip Art is a creative storytelling frame that reveals multiple images in a sequence using a sliding visual mechanism. Built from eco-friendly materials, it shares layered stories through artwork—combining sustainability, beauty, and purpose in a single frame.

Status: Running

View Project
Solo Steam

Solo Steam is an AI-powered tool designed to help music enthusiasts master lead melodies and harmonies on their instruments. It listens, analyzes, and teaches users through intuitive, personalized feedback—making music learning accessible and adaptive.

Status: In Research

Recent Activities

IT Students Join the OpenBox Dev Team

January 10, 2025

challenge, OpenBox

A team of IT students has officially joined the OpenBox development initiative at our Ile-Ife Research Unit. The collaboration aims to speed up deployment while providing students hands-on experience in building smart storage and document systems that serve underserved communities.

Flip Art Wins Ife Art Challenge

October 12, 2024

challenge, Flip Art

Flip Art, our storytelling art frame project, emerged as the winner of the Ife Art Challenge 2036. The piece stood out for its eco-friendly design and creative ability to display multiple stories in a single frame. This recognition is a major boost for our sustainable art outreach.
